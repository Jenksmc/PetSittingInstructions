Opening & closing the feeders

You only need one button to refill: the Open/Close button. It's the largest button on the feeder. Looking at the front of the feeder it's the button furthest to the right; looking at the back (where the buttons actually are) it's the one furthest to the left.
1

On the back of the feeder, press the biggest button (furthest left when you're behind it). The lid slides open.

2

Lift out the old bowl and grab a fresh or rinsed one. Fill it: 1 can (Juliet, Gloria) or 2 cans (Agosto).

3

Set the bowl back in the feeder, flat on its tray.

4

Press the same Open/Close button again. The lid closes and seals the food fresh. Safe to press even while a cat is eating: it won't close on them, it'll wait and close once they're done.

⚠️ Don't press the "Add a pet" button (the smaller one with the cat + icon). That puts the feeder in programming mode and the light will start flashing green. If you hit it by accident, just press it once more to cancel. Also: if you leave the lid open too long it will close itself after about 15 minutes. Just press Open/Close again.
